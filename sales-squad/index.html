How to Interpret Your Data
  • Leads → Appts conversion below 20%? Your messaging or qualifying process may need tightening. Focus on lead quality over quantity.
  • Show Rate below 80%? Prospects are dropping off before meetings. Send reminders, confirm value upfront, and reduce the gap between booking and attending.
  • Appts → Sales conversion below 25%? Review your sales process. Are you qualifying properly before the meeting? Is your offer clear and compelling?
  • Revenue on target but units behind? You're closing higher-value deals but fewer of them. Consider whether volume or value matters more for your goals.
  • Units on target but revenue behind? You're selling volume at lower prices. Look at upselling, bundling, or repositioning your offer at a higher price point.
  • All metrics below target? Start at the top of the funnel. More leads in means more opportunities throughout. Increase outreach before optimising conversions.
  • Use the "View As" filter to project your current pace over different time frames. If weekly numbers look good but monthly targets are off, your consistency may need work.
  • Filter by product to identify which products or services are performing and which need attention. Double down on what works, fix or drop what doesn't.
